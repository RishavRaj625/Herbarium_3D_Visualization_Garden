Some common questions
were often asked

Which can plants improve digestive health?

Plants like Triphala, Ajwain (Carom seeds), and Bael are recognized for their digestive benefits. Triphala aids in bowel regulation, Ajwain helps alleviate indigestion, and Bael supports gut health by reducing inflammation in the intestines.

Which plants are best for boosting immunity?

Tulsi (Holy Basil), Ashwagandha, and Giloy are well-known for their immune-boosting properties. Tulsi acts as an antioxidant, Ashwagandha helps the body adapt to stress, and Giloy improves immune response by detoxifying the system.

What are some natural plants for skin care?

Plants like Neem, Turmeric, and Aloe Vera for skin care. Neem purifies the skin, Turmeric has anti-inflammatory properties, and Aloe Vera soothes and hydrates, promoting overall skin health.

Can these be grown at home, and how to use?

Plants such as Tulsi, Aloe Vera, and Neem, can be easily grown at home. Tulsi can be used in teas to boost immunity, Aloe Vera can be applied topically for skin care or consumed for digestive health, and Neem leaves can be used for skin treatments or as a natural¬†disinfectant
